Do not stop at reading

The useful question is not how to use AI everywhere. It is which repeated work should stop consuming human time.

Good AI and automation targets

  • Document analysis and extraction
  • Question generation and learning support
  • Task flow automation
  • Analytics summaries and decision support
  • Content generation with human review

The best targets are repeated, structured, and measurable.
