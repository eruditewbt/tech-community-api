Collaboration Rules

Good collaboration is structured contribution with shared goals.

01
State the problem clearly
Nobody can help a vague idea. Make the need visible.
02
Show your role and availability
Say what you can do and what you need from others.
03
Choose a shared outcome
Teams should align on what visible proof counts as success.
04
Work in short sprints
Small visible progress beats long invisible planning.

How To Join A Working Team

Post your intent, match to a role, then move into a sprint.

Step 1
Post intent

Say what you want to achieve, what field you are in, and what kind of help you need.

Step 2
Match to a role

Choose a role that fits your current strength and the team's current need.

Step 3
Join a sprint

Move from conversation into a visible task with a deadline and output.
