What counts as proof

  • a working demo
  • a repo with a useful change
  • a sprint page with completion state
  • a write-up that explains the outcome
  • a reusable asset that others can build on

What progress should feel like

  • visible
  • specific
  • repeatable
  • public enough to trust
  • small enough to finish
