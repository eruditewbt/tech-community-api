Field guide

How to use technology to strengthen the field you already belong to

A practical explanation for healthcare, education, business, law, design, and other non-software fields on where tech creates leverage.

Do not stop at reading

You do not need to abandon your field to benefit from tech. You need to learn where data, automation, interfaces, and collaboration improve the work.

Where tech adds leverage

  • Healthcare: data flow, intake systems, scheduling, analytics, education tools.
  • Education: content generation, assessment, task management, student support workflows.
  • Business: dashboards, internal tools, decision automation, customer systems.
  • Law and policy: document analysis, search, compliance workflows, secure information handling.

How collaboration works here

A domain expert defines the real need. An engineer builds the system. A designer shapes the experience. An operator makes it usable in the real world. That is one of the strongest differentiators of this community.

  • Bring the problem from your field.
  • Translate it into a proposal.
  • Join a project with engineers and designers.
If you want to propose a field-specific system, use the Ideas page and then post your intent in the discussion channels.
