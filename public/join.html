What to do in your first day

  • subscribe on YouTube
  • choose a path
  • open the repo that matches your goal

Post Your Intent Directly

This sends your intent into the live platform backend so it can show up in the admin dashboard and community operations flow.

Also post in Discord

Your submitted intent can power role matching and project routing.

What happens next

  • your intent is stored for admin review
  • it can be used to match you to a project lane
  • it gives the community a live signal of what people want
  • you should still join Discord for the fastest human follow-up
